Coimbatore Power Cut Announcement – Nov 13, 2025

Published: Wednesday, November 12, 2025, 16:48 IST

Over 270 locations including many parts of Coimbatore will experience a scheduled power outage on Thursday, November 13 due to maintenance work by the Tamil Nadu Generation and Distribution Corporation.

Affected Areas in Coimbatore:

  • Bharathi Colony, Peelamedu Pudhur, Sowripalayam, Nanjundapuram Road
  • Puliyakulam, Ganapathy Industrial Estate, Avarampalayam
  • Ramanathapuram, Kallimadi, Trichy Road (Part), Meena Estate
  • Udayampalayam, Sarkarsamakulam, Kovilpalayam, Kurumbapalayam
  • Mannikampalayam, Agrakara Samakulam, Kondaiyampalayam
  • Kunnathur, Kallipalayam, Mondikalipudur
